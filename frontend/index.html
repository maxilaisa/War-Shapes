SHAPE ARENA

Player 1 (WASD)

W/S - Move Up/Down | A/D - Move Left/Right

Q - Light Ability | E - Medium Ability | R - Heavy Ability

F - Activate Ultimate | Space - Dodge

Player 2 (Arrow Keys)

Arrow Keys - Movement

/ - Light Ability | . - Medium Ability | ' - Heavy Ability

Enter - Activate Ultimate | Shift - Dodge

Reduce opponent HP to 0 or knock them out of bounds to win!

Build combos to increase damage and charge your ultimate.
